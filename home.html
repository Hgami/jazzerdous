Boston Based Jazz Band

We love music and so do you. Time pruce up your nightlife! Come see us, we play all over the Boston area.


THE BAND


We are five-person jazz band with an eclectic sound. Our inspirations stem from country to classical and jazz. We fuel our music with an assortment of inspirations, ranging from classical sounds to traditional blues. We are influenced by all of the great jazz artists, present and past, but we also aim to create unique music which resonates with our spirit and our listeners. We cherish Boston's lively music scene, which keeps us grounded in our roots as well as new inspiration.

The members of the band include Brian Wilson as the lead vocalist and playing the Electric Guitar, Dan Johnson who plays the drums, Pete Williams on the Piano, Sam Davis on the Saxophone as well as the trumpet and Bob Miller plays the upright bass. All the band members live in the greater Boston Area and are proficient with playing more than one musical instrument and are also the back-up vocals for their songs.


Learn more about our songs by reading our lyrics and listening to our tunes. We would love to see you at a concert. Thank you for listening and for tuning in!

INSTRUMENTS

Most of us play multiple instruments but we do have our favorites.

Saxophone

The saxophone is used in classical music (such as concert bands, chamber music, solo repertoire, and, occasionally, orchestras), military bands, marching bands, and jazz (such as big bands and jazz combos)..

Drums

Drums is a collection of drums and other percussion instruments, typically cymbals, which are set up on stands to be played by a single player,

Trumpet

A trumpet is a brass instrument commonly used in classical and jazz ensembles. The trumpet group contains the instruments with the highest register in the brass family.

Electric guitar

An electric guitar is a guitar that uses one or more pickups to convert the vibration of its strings into electrical signals. The vibration occurs when a guitar player strums, plucks, fingerpicks, slaps or taps the strings

Upright Bass

The double bass, or simply the bass (and numerous other names), is the largest and lowest-pitched bowed string instrument in the modern symphony orchestra
